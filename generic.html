Contract Lifecycle Management

Contract lifecycle management (CLM) automates and streamlines contract processes during key stages. These stages include initiation, authoring, process and workflow, negotiation and approval, execution, ongoing management and compliance (within the repository), and contract renewal. Dolphin has been implemnted to take care of the CLM process. Please find the FAQ document in the 'Resources' section.

Storage Digitization

Digital storage or Record Management System has been implemented across all legal entities. This project includes converting all physical documents into soft copies and moving all the physical documents to a centralized warehouse. All the documents will be available on cloud and hard copies will be available on request within a specified SLA. This will help the organization in overall digitization and will create ease of access for the content at the same time it is expected to increase operational efficiency for accessing the records. We have partnered with Iron Mountain a fortune 500 company to drive the digital storage initiative. The implementation is aimed at reducing manual intervention in record storage and ease of access for the records.

Managed Printing Services

This initiative has been implemented across all legal entities and have enabled secure printing. All print jobs are stored in a users personal secure print queue until they are released. The printers allow default double-sided, highspeed printouts with the flexibility to take printouts from anywhere. It promotes responsible power and paper consumption, also as a green initiative Canon has planted 40 trees for UPES. All the printers are equipped with original toners and 100 % Toner availability is ensured with the help of remote management tool.

Digital Signage

Digital signage is a segment of electronic signage using digital technologies such as LCD, LED, projection, and e-paper to display digital images and text. Implementation have encouraged replacement of manual approvals with system-based approvals for transparency and accountability. Content is created using MagicInfo platform of Samsung which enhances the UI/UX thereby creating a positive experience for the students and getting out quick information and important communications. The initiative has resulted in reduction of paper prints and contributed to the green initiative project.
